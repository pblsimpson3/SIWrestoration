# The Saugus Iron Works Restoration App
This app is an educational prototype. Please check that it works on your classroom devices, as performance may vary based on your local network and device settings.

## Why and how this capstone project exists

I, [Robert Simpson](www.linkedin.com/in/rsimpson3), completed my AI Professional Certificate on 6/12/26. The app brings to life a historical documentary film lesson plan where students observe, evaluate and reflect on teacher selected narration quotes from the 1950s produced film. Students judge whether the quote applies 1 of 4 roles. Upon clicking **Yes Likely** they MUST write a comment on why the quote aligns to their role.

### Tips & Known Issues
- Playing the film automatically syncs to 19 quotes. Use the *Pause at Quote end* feature to auto stop at end of quote. Sometimes the last quote word may cut off. 
- Students can rate the quotes 1 by 1 while playing through the film. 
- Students should activate **subtitles/cc** on the YouTube stream. 
- Not possible to hide YouTube branding and channel information. I agree this can be distracting for some users when pausing the video. 
- Use the **Film Quotes Timeline** to jump the playhead to the start of each quote.
- Students are NOT required to add their name or email. 
- Export: Word .doc export produced a file that doesn't open on MAC OS programs such as Pages. 
- Export: Print / Save as PDF is the most reliable export option
- Export: Export to Google Drive not functional in v1.0
